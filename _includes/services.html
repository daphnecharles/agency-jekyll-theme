Services

We help companies create inclusive communities.

Training and Facilitation

We offer anti-racist and implicit bias diversity trainings to help employees understand and fight systematic discrimination.

Recruitment Redesign

We analyze and improve every internal process in your recruitment methodology to attract talent from all backgrounds.

ERG Development

Employee resource groups are an important aspect of creating an inclusive atmosphere at your company. We help develop ERGs and various programming and events.

Pay Equity Audits

We carefully analyze every individual's complete salary package including base salary, signing bonus, target bonus, equity (options or RSUs) using regression analysis. Our goal is to find discrepancies across the same level, and then to find discrepancies in various demographics across levels.

Diversity Outreach and Candidate Sourcing

With professional connections to organizations such as Out in Tech, Women Who Code, Code2040, and HIREBLACK we provide visibility of your job openings to diverse candidates.
